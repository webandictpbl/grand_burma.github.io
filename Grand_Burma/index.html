Myanmar

Myanmar (Burma) is a Southeast Asian country with over 55 million people. The largest city in Myanmar is called Yangon and it is also the most popluar city in Myanmar.
nearly 90% of population are Buddhist. The most popular Pagoda in Myanmar is Shwedagon and it is also located in Yangon. Myanmar is also bordering India, Bangladesh,
China, Laos and Thailand. Myanmar flag has Yellow, Green, Red in the background and a White star in the middle representing unity, conformity, wisdom, happiness, fairness, peaceful, pleasant, bravery and decisiveness.
