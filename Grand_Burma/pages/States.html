Chin State




Chin is a state located in the western of Myanmar.
It's 36019 square kilometer and boredered by
Sagaing Division. The population of Chin state is
about 478801. There are many places to go in Chin
such as Nat Ma Taung National park, Kanpetlet,
Chin State Cultural Museum and more.

Kachin State




Kachin is a state located in the northernmost of
Myanmar. It is 89041 kilometer square. Kachin state
has Myanmar's highest mountain Hkakabo Razi
(5889 meters). There are many placese to go in Kachin
state such as Indawgyi Lake, Khakaborazi National
Park, Indawgyi lake wildlife sanctuary and etc.


Kayah State




Kayah State is a state of Myanmar, which is located in eastern
Myanmar. It is also the smallest state in Myanmar. the area of
Kayah State is 11,732 km² The triditional food of Kayah is
Kayah sausage. The capital city of Kayah is Loikaw which has
the area of 1,549 km². The famous places in Kayah are Sri
Mingala Taung Taw (Taung Kwe Zedi), Kyat Cave, Aung Tha
Pyae Cave and many more

Kayin State




Kayin State, also known as Karen State, is a state of Myanmar. The
capital city is Hpa-An which has the population of 51,820. some
people called the Kayin State as the land of the caves. The kayin
states has the area of 30,383 km². The most famous places in kayin
state are Kaw Gon Cave, Kaw Ka Thaung Cave, Mount Zwegabin
and many more.


Mon State




Mon State is an division of Myanmar with the land area of
12,155 km².Mon state has a capital of Mawlamyine, the fourth
largest city in Myanmar which has the area of 218.9 km². The
triditional food Mon State is Thingyan Rice. Some famous
places in Mon are Kyaik Htee Yoe Pagoda, Win Sein Tawya
(which is one of the largest reclining Buddha in the world),
Zin Kyaik Waterfall and many others.

Rakhine State




Rakhine State is a state in Myanmar with the area of 36,778 km².
The population of Rakhine is about 3.2 million. The capital city
of Rakhine is Sittwe with the population of around 200,000.
The triditional food of Rakhine are Ngar Ni Tuu Thoke, Gone
Thauk Sann, and Pinlalsar Mar Lar Shan Kaw.The famous
places in Rakhine are Ngapali Beach, Lawkananda Pagoda,
Shitthaung Paya and many more.


Shan State




Shan state is the biggest state in myanmar with the area of 155,801 km².The population of the Shan state is about 5.6 million. The triditional food of shan state are Nga Htamin and Shan noodle. The famous place in Shan states are Inle Lake, Mway taw kakku, Pindaya Caves and many more.
